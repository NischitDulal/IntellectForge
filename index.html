    FAQs

    1. How do I schedule a tutoring session?

    To schedule a tutoring session, you can visit our "Plans&Package" page after you have a subscription and choose a suitable time slot. Alternatively, you can contact our support team for assistance.

    2. What subjects do you offer tutoring for?

    We offer tutoring for a wide range of subjects, including Math, Physics, English, Chemistry, and more. Check our "Subjects" page for a detailed list.

    3. What is the pricing for tutoring sessions?

    Our pricing varies based on the subject and duration of the tutoring session. Please refer to our "Pricing" page for detailed information on our tutoring packages and rates.

    4. Can I choose a specific tutor?

    Yes, you can choose a specific tutor based on their expertise and availability. During the scheduling process, you will have the option to select your preferred tutor.
